About Me

I always had an interest in music since a young age, from playing trumpet in jazz band to marching band. It wasn't until my first roadying job, at the age of 15, did I discover my passion for DJing. In 2011 I started roadying with various DJ companies all over Long Island. I was shown techniques and tricks of the trade that I continued to expand on on my own. While working as a roadie I decided to invest in DJ equipment so I could start practicing at home. Shortly after, I started booking my own events which consisted of sweet sixteens, bar mitzvahs and small backyard parties. As time went on I continued getting booked for more events of different venue types. In 2016 I made my way to the bar/club scene where I got to explore a totally different style of DJing, a more free-wheeling high energy style. My main focus was always private events but I did enjoy Djing in the nightlife scene on occasion. Having DJ-ed in both the private event industry as well as the nightlife scene, I always try to blend the two styles as much as possible. I always cater to the client and their guests while bringing a high energy mixing style to every party. One of my favorite things as a DJ is looking up from the booth and seeing everyone on the dancefloor showing off their moves and dancing the night away.

We serve our clients through a number of services, including DJing, MCing, photography, lighting and party planning.
